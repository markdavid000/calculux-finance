Four Pillars of Calculux

Ecosystem

Non-Custodial

Interact directly from your web3 wallet app. Unlike centralized platfoms, Calculux doesn’t hold your assets when you connect (It is NonCustodian): you have 100% ownership of your own assets.

Affordable and Boundless Solution for Decentralization

Calculux Finance runs on Binance Smart Chain, a blockchain that is scalabe, with much lower transaction costs than Ethereum or Bitcoin. Swap and minting fees are lower than other platforms.

Unparallel Access To Decentralized Finance

People from certain region across the globe still can’t access most Centralized Exchanges for their day to day transactions. Calculux Ecosystem is decentralized which makes it possible for anyone anywhere around the world to access and utilize our platform. Calculux is built for the people to help them achieve their financial freedom

DAO

Calculux ecosystem is decentralized, the decisions on community development and operrations will be openly carried out by $CAX holders through voting and not influenced by any centralized entity.
